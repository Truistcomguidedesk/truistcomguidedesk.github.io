Activate Your Card Easily

Activating your card is an important step before you can begin using it for purchases, online payments, and ATM withdrawals. The activation process is simple and usually takes only a few minutes to complete. By following the steps below, you can ensure that your card is ready to use without any issues.

Always make sure you are using the official activation page and a secure internet connection while entering your details. This helps protect your information and ensures a safe activation process.

Step-by-Step Activation Process

Step 1

Visit the official activation page using your browser.

Step 2

Enter your card number and required details carefully.

Step 3

Verify your identity using OTP or security questions.

Step 4

Submit your details and complete the activation process.

Why Activation is Important

Once your card is activated, you can start using it for secure transactions. Activation ensures that your card is linked to your account and protected against unauthorized use. It also enables features such as online payments, contactless transactions, and ATM access.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does activation take?
The process usually takes less than 2 minutes.

Can I activate using mobile?
Yes, you can activate your card using any smartphone or tablet.

Is the process secure?
Yes, as long as you use the official website and a secure connection.
